Dear prospective student!

Arniko is a dynamic college community which "dares to be different".We constantly ask the question, "How can we do better?"

Arniko is sharpening its tool to face the challenges. We dream about a world where quality is the buzz word. We have happily blended knowledge with wisdom; personality with character; Science with life. We are moving in tune with the need of the hour and the future days.

Aims and Objectives

It aims to nurture the innate potential of today's youth to mold them into responsible bona fide citizens. Millions of rupees are drained out from Nepal in search of quality education in India and overseas. The running of this college is inspired by nationalistic feeling. We are committed to provide full satisfaction in the minimum cost capable of coping with the challenges of the complex modern world.
